Network Planning

College Project | 2024

For this project, I designed detailed network layouts to optimize connectivity and efficiency, creating both physical and logical diagrams tailored to real-world scenarios.

Approach

  • Studied network requirements like device placement and traffic flow.
  • Developed physical diagrams showing hardware locations and cabling.
  • Created logical diagrams with IP addressing, subnets, and VLAN configurations.
  • Simulated the design to ensure scalability and performance.

Tools Used

Cisco Packet Tracer, Visio, Networking Protocols (TCP/IP, VLANs)

Outcome

Produced a blueprint for a high-performing network, ready to support a small campus or office environment.
